Unit 9: Acidity and Alkalinity

1. pH Scale Explorer

Select a pH value...
Acids (pH 0-6):
The smaller the pH value, the more acidic the substance is.
Alkalis (pH 8-14):
The larger the pH value, the more alkaline the substance is.

2. Comparison Sentence Patterns

Comparing Acids

A (pH 1) vs B (pH 3)

Style 1: A is more acidic than B.
Style 2: B is less acidic than A.
Style 3: A has a higher acidity than B.
Style 4: B has a lower acidity than A.

Comparing Alkalis

D (pH 13) vs C (pH 9)

Style 1: D is more alkaline than C.
Style 2: C is less alkaline than D.
Style 3: D has a higher alkalinity than C.
Style 4: C has a lower alkalinity than D.
